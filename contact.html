FAQ

How do you select creators for campaigns?

We use a combination of data analysis and personal relationships. We look at audience demographics, engagement quality, and content style — then match based on brand fit, not just follower count.

What's your typical project timeline?

Most campaigns take 4–6 weeks from brief to live. Strategy-only projects can be completed in 2 weeks. We'll always agree on timelines upfront so there are no surprises.

Do you work with brands outside the Netherlands?

Yes. While we're based in Amsterdam and have deep roots in the Dutch creator space, we work with brands across Europe and beyond.
